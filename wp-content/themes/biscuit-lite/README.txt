== Description ==

Biscuit Lite is a WordPress theme for food bloggers. Lightweight and highly extendable. Very fast, responsive, RTL & translation ready and best SEO practices. Your site will looks good on every device: mobile, tablet and desktop. It's also retina compatible.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I use on WordPress.com? = 
Yes, but only with Business Plan.
